The product: You send a proposal to a potential client, a resume to a potential employer or an interesting file to a friend...and never get feedback! With Share|Connect, you give the receiver the ability to send you instant messages while they are reviewing the document. Whether the potential customer has a question, the employer want to discuss your experience or your friend likes to say thanks: you are online to chat with them in real-time. Even if they decide not to contact you, at least you will know that they opened the document with instant tracking notifications in your account. So much better than having files disappear into the email inbox black hole. Express your interest by preordering above or sign up for the newsletter!

What is all this about: This project is crowdfunded product/market fit test, created for the >Startup Engineering class at Stanford<. The main goal of this website is to test whether there is sufficient interest in this product to evaluate whether I should go ahead and develop it. Put differently, this crowdsourcing project seeks to test potential interest in the Share|Connect product. The money raised goes toward development of the beta product which I estimate to take 6 months, likely quicker if I raise more than the minimum amount. Once complete, you will gain access to the product for 3 months, after which users can continue to use it for a small fee. This is meant to be a low-price/low-cost product.

FAQ

How is the funding goal calculated?
My estimate for the monthly price of the product is $5/month so by contributing $10 you pay for two months and get one month free to compensate you for having faith in my abilities and taking the risk. I estimate at least 1,000 people would need to be interested in purchasing this product for me to go ahead.
What happens with my money?
Your small contribution is essentially an upfront payment for using the beta version of Share|Connect once it is developed. In return for using an innovative product, you are taking the risk that I might not get it developed. Given my expertise in web development and management, I hope to give give you sufficient comfort that I can bring the project to completion. And if I do not reach the desired funding goal I will refund the money you sent!
Why BitCoin?
Traditionally, crowdfunding happened through well-known platforms such as Kickstarter and indiegogo. Bitcoins allows to crowdfund projects without the need for a platform.
